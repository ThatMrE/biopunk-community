Community = the movement

The best biotech founders are hidden in labs and universities — invisible to capital until it's almost too late. Biopunk finds them earlier, gives them a house to live in, a lab to build in, and a check to start with.

We're not an accelerator with a Slack channel. We're a movement: a real-world network of co-living houses, shared wet labs, grants, office hours, and Tuesday dinners — wired together by a fund that owns the cap table early and backs people, not just decks. Community is the connective tissue across everything below.
